Consignment

Apart from retailing shoes, InSole also accepts second hand shoes, as long as conditions are met.

Consignments provides customers a cheap and easy way to earn money, as well as providing others with cheaper shoes.

Here's how you can consign:

  1. Fill in a form located below to list the shoes that you are intending to sell.
  2. Bring your shoes to our store at 89 Desker Rd, Singapore 209611.
  3. Wait for a few days as we verify the legitimacy of your shoes.
  4. You will receive your payment through your preferred method.
